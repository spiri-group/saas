## 11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

### 11.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption at rest:** All data stored in Azure Cosmos DB and Azure Blob Storage is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher
- **Payment security:** Payment card data is processed by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. We never store full card numbers, CVVs, or PINs on our servers
- **Access controls:** Role-based access controls restrict data access to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis
- **Authentication:** Secure authentication mechanisms for user accounts, including security questions for account recovery
- **Infrastructure security:** Azure platform security features including network isolation, DDoS protection, and intrusion detection

### 11.2 Organisational Measures
- Staff training on data protection and information security
- Data processing agreements with all third-party processors
- Regular review of data access permissions
- Incident response procedures for data breach notification
- Privacy impact assessments for new features and processing activities

### 11.3 Breach Notification
In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

- **UK:** Notify the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay where required under UK GDPR
- **Australia:** Notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) and affected individuals as required under the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme
- **United States:** Notify affected individuals and relevant state authorities in accordance with applicable state breach notification laws
