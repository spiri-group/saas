## Special Considerations for Spiritual Merchants

### Seasonal Cycles Matter
Spiritual businesses have unique rhythms:
- **Full moons & new moons**: High-demand times for crystals, candles, ritual tools
- **Solstices & equinoxes**: Workshop and retreat peaks
- **Mercury retrograde**: Both high sales (protection tools) and communication challenges
- **Holiday seasons**: Gift-giving for spiritual tools

**Marketing Approach**: Time campaigns around these cycles. "Full moon sale starting‚Äîdon't let sold-out stop you" messaging.

### Community Over Competition
Spiritual entrepreneurs often support each other rather than compete.

**Marketing Approach**: Emphasize marketplace discovery ("your customers might find you while shopping with another lightworker"), collaboration features, community aspects.

### Pricing Sacred Work
Many spiritual practitioners struggle with charging for their gifts.

**Marketing Approach**: Normalize abundance. "Your gifts are valuable. Thriving financially allows you to serve more people. Fair exchange is sacred."

### Trust & Safety
Spiritual seekers can be vulnerable. They need to feel safe.

**Marketing Approach**: Emphasize buyer protection, clear policies, merchant verification, community standards.
